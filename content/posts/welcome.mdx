I built this site as a place to think in public about software engineering. The
bar I've set for myself is simple: **publish only what I would have wanted to
read a year ago.**

## What you'll find here

Expect practical, opinionated writing on:

- Distributed systems and the boring tradeoffs that matter in production
- Clean architecture — used as a tool, not a religion
- Developer experience and the small rituals that compound over years
- Notes from shipping real systems, not toy projects

## How to follow along

- Subscribe via [RSS](/feed.xml) — the most durable way
- Or drop your email on the [contact page](/contact) and I'll let you know when
  something new goes up

Thanks for reading. — Taynan
